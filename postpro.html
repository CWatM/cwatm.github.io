Time depending and non depending output maps¶

Output maps will be produced as netcdf4 maps, stack of maps (over time)

Windows

For netCDF: Panoply

Linux

For netCDF: ncview

Timeseries¶

timeseries are procuded as ASCII files, which can be read with every text editor
